Notes

  • Single‑phase, no phase change, constant properties, steady state.
  • Ċ = ṁ·Cp with SI bases: ṁ in kg/s, Cp in J/(kg·K) gives W/K.
  • NTU = U·A/Cmin. For 1–1:
    • Counter‑current: ε = [1 − exp(−NTU·(1 − Cr))] ÷ [1 − Cr·exp(−NTU·(1 − Cr))]
    • Co‑current: ε = [1 − exp(−NTU·(1 + Cr))] ÷ [1 + Cr]
  • Outlets from energy balance: Th,out = Th,in − Q/Ċh, Tc,out = Tc,in + Q/Ċc.
  • Extend later for P–P arrangements and fouling resistances if needed.
